The Secret Identity of Prince Adam

Adam was born Prince of Eternia. He is the son of King Randor and Queen Marlena (an Earthling).

His twin sister, Adora, was kidnapped at birth and raised by Hordak (leader of the Evil Horde) on Etheria.

Although a rather lackadaisical individual, Adam's life and the life of his aptly-named friend Cringer (a green tiger),

changed drastically the day he received the Sword of Power from Teela Na, Sorceress of Castle Grayskull.

With this Sword, and by the mystical power of Castle Grayskull, Prince Adam is changed into He-Man, the most powerful man in the universe.

His friend Cringer is also changed into the mighty Battle Cat. And thus, their adventures begin.
