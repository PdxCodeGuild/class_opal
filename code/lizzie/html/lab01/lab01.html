Kaedehara Kazuha

“Come driving rain or winds that churn, I shall return, by blade alone, armed, if barefoot, to my home…”

Introduction:

A wandering samurai from Inazuma who is currently with Liyue's Crux Fleet. A gentle and carefree soul whose heart hides a great many burdens from the past.

This young man's calm personality belies a razor sharp blade that has seen many battles. Who knows if it was the storms of life that smoothed out his rough edges, or if his natural restraint has kept his edge sheathed...
— Genshin official website

Talents & Abilities:

  • Normal Attack: Garyuu Bladework
  • Elemental Skill: Chihayaburu
  • Plunging Attack: Midare ranzan
  • Elemental Burst: Kazuha Slash
  • 1st Ascension Passive: Soumon Swordsmanship
  • 4th Ascension Passive: Poetics of Fuubutsu
